---
title: "Why verify Skills?"
---

Skills listed on a profile are often self-declared, making it difficult for recruiters and employers to determine whether a candidate truly possesses the expertise they claim. Skill Verification transforms self-reported skills into trusted, evidence-backed credentials that can be confidently evaluated during hiring decisions.

Verified skills are supported by real-world proof, assessments, or professional endorsements, providing stronger credibility than traditional resumes alone. Each verification creates a trusted record that demonstrates how the skill was earned and validated.

## Benefits of Skill Verification

### Build Trust and Credibility

Verified skills provide independent validation of your abilities, helping recruiters trust your profile with confidence.

### Stand Out from Other Candidates

Candidates with verified skills are more likely to attract attention because their expertise is supported by proof rather than claims.

### Showcase Real-World Experience

Skills can be verified through work experience, projects, certifications, assessments, and learning activities, highlighting practical application rather than theoretical knowledge.

### Improve Recruiter Visibility

Verified skills increase profile strength and help recruiters identify qualified candidates more efficiently.

### Create a Portable Skill Record

All verified skills are stored in the Skill Repository, creating a permanent record of your achievements, endorsements, assessments, and supporting evidence.

### Demonstrate Continuous Learning

Skills earned through assessments, certifications, and learning activities showcase your commitment to professional growth and upskilling.

## Trusted Proof of Capability

Whether earned through Human Endorsement, Assessment Verification, Certifications, or Learning Verification, each verified skill acts as a trusted proof point that helps employers make better hiring decisions and helps candidates showcase their expertise with confidence.
