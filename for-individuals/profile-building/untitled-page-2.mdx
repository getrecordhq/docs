## Adding a Licence or Certificate

To add a certificate:

1. Go to **Profile → Licence & Certificates → Add Certificate**
2. Fill in the required fields
3. Save and request verification
4. Complete any required assessment (if skill is linked)

**Licence / Certificate Name:**

Enter the exact name printed on your certificate.

**Credential ID:**

Optional unique ID printed on your certificate.

**Credential URL:**

Add the public verification link provided by the issuing platform.

**Issued Date:**

Select the month and year when you received the certificate.

**Expiry Date:**

Some certificates have validity (e.g., AWS, RedHat, Scrum).

Add the expiry date if applicable.

If your certificate does not expire, enable **“No expiry.”**

**Skill Eligibility**

Some certifications are linked to **skills** that you can earn by completing a short test generated by our AI.

### How it works:

1. After your certificate is verified, you’ll be eligible to take a related assessment.
2. Complete the **assessment within 15 minutes**.
3. After passing, you will earn the **matching skills** that gets added to your profile.

<Info>
  This process helps recruiters identify the actual skills you possess based on real, verified certifications.
</Info>

## Why Add Certificates?

Adding your certifications shows recruiters the work you've done and the knowledge you've gained. It helps you:

- **Build trust** by showing verified credentials
- **Earn skills** that showcase your expertise
- **Increase visibility** for relevant job matches
- **Stand out** in recruiter searches

<Info>
  A verified and badge-enabled certificate helps increase your profile's strength and gives recruiters a clearer understanding of your skills
</Info>
