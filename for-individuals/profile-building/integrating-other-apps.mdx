## How to Connect Integrations

To connect your external platform accounts:

1. Go to **Profile → Integrations**
2. Select the platform you want to connect
3. Enter your username or profile URL
4. Click **Connect**
5. Follow the on-screen instructions to authorize the connection

## GitHub Integration

**Showcase your repositories, contributions, and activity heatmap.**

- Enter your **GitHub username** or **URL**:

  _Example_: https://github.com/username
- Click **Connect** to sync your GitHub activity with your profile.

## Dribbble Integration

**Display your creative designs and build a strong visual portfolio.**

- Enter your **Dribbble username** or **URL**:

  _Example_: https://dribbble.com/username
- Click **Connect** to display your Dribbble portfolio on your profile.

## LeetCode Integration

**Showcase your global rank, stats, and submission graph.**

- Enter your **LeetCode username** or **URL**:

  _Example_: https://leetcode.com/u/username
- Click **Connect** to show your coding skills and problem-solving achievements.

## HackerRank Integration

**Display your coding skills, badges, and problem-solving achievements.**

- Enter your **HackerRank profile URL**:

  _Example_: https://www.hackerrank.com/profile/username
- Click **Connect** to add your HackerRank profile to your Record profile.

## Why Integrate Your Accounts?

Integrating your accounts provides several benefits:

- **Showcase your coding achievements** (GitHub, LeetCode, HackerRank)
- **Display your creative work** (Dribbble)
- **Earn credibility** with verified contributions
- **Enhance your profile’s visibility** to recruiters looking for skilled professionals
