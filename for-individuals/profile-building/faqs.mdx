---
title: "FAQs"
description: "Here are the most common questions related to the Profile Section to help guide you through creating, editing, and verifying your profile details."
---

<Expandable title="How do I create or edit my about section in profile?">
  Go to **Profile → Edit Profile** and fill out the fields like **Personal Details**, **Contact Details**, **Summary**, **Language** and **Social Profiles**. You can edit your profile information at any time.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Why is the “Summary” field important?">
  The **Summary** section lets you describe your skills, background, and career aspirations. This helps recruiters understand who you are beyond just your qualifications.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I leave some fields empty in my profile?">
  Yes, you can leave fields like **Grade**, **Location**, or **Marital Status** empty if you prefer. However, a fully completed profile increases your chances of being discovered by recruiters.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How can I add work experience?">
  Go to **Profile → Career → Add Work Experience**. Enter details like **Job Title**, **Company Name**, **Start/End Date**, and **Skills** used during the role. You can also upload relevant documents.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I add education details?">
  Go to **Profile → Career → Add Education**. Fill in your **School/College Name**, **Degree Type**, **Field of Study**, and **Start/End Dates**. You can also add a **Description** of your studies or projects.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What is the verification process for work experience and education?">
  Once you add **Work Experience** or **Education**, you can request verification. For **Work Experience**, the verifier must be an official from your workplace (HR, manager). For **Education**, the verifier must be a professor or administrative staff from your educational institution. Verification helps improve the credibility of your profile.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I add a certificate or license?">
  Go to **Profile → Licence & Certificates → Add Certificate**. Enter the certificate name, **Issued By**, **Credential ID**, and upload the certificate document. For some certificates, you may be able to take a short test to earn a **matching skill badge**.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What is the purpose of skill badges?">
  Skill badges are linked to your **verified certificates** and **work experience**. They help recruiters quickly see your proficiency in specific skills. Some certifications may require a short assessment to unlock the badge.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I add multiple certifications or licenses?">
  Yes, you can add multiple certificates. Each one is tied to a specific **Skill Badge** if applicable, and all certificates are stored in your **Profile → Licence & Certificates** section.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I add activities like volunteering or sports?">
  Go to **Profile → Projects & Activities → Add Activity**. You can add **Activity Name**, **Organization/Venue**, **Start/End Dates**, and a **Description** of your involvement. Activities help highlight your extra-curricular contributions.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Do I need to verify my integrated accounts?">
  No, integration accounts (like GitHub, LeetCode) do not require verification. Simply link your account to showcase your achievements directly in your profile.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I edit or remove my integrated accounts later?">
  Yes, you can edit or remove any integrated accounts by going back to the **Profile → Integrations** section and updating the connection settings.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What should I do if the verifier didn’t receive the verification email?">
  Ask the verifier to check their **Spam** or **Promotions** folder. If they still can’t find the email, you can resend the verification request from your profile.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I delete my profile or any specific details?">
  Yes, you can delete your entire profile or individual sections like **Work Experience**, **Education**, **Certificates**, or **Activities** at any time.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Do I need to verify every piece of information I add to my profile?">
  No, verification is only required for **Work Experience**, **Education**, and certain **Licenses/Certificates**. Activities or skills do not require verification.
</Expandable>
