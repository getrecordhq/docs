title: "Referral Program "
---

The Referral Program allows you to invite friends to join Record and earn **3 free months of Pro** when they complete their profile setup. It’s the easiest way to unlock premium features while helping others start their learning journey.

## **1. How to Invite Friends**

You can invite anyone using your unique referral link.

### **Where to find your referral link**

1. Click your **Profile Icon** (top-right).
2. Select **Refer Friends**.
3. Under the **Invite** tab, copy your unique invite link.

### **Ways to Share**

You can share your link instantly via:

- WhatsApp
- X (Twitter)
- Instagram
- LinkedIn
- Or simply copy and send it anywhere

## **2. How Rewards Work**

Both you **and your friend** receive rewards.

### **You get rewards when:**

- Your invited friend registers using your link
- They complete their **profile setup** inside Record

### **Reward You Earn**

- **1 Month of Pro** added to your plan automatically

## **3. Tracking Your Referrals**

The **Rewards** tab shows your referral performance.

You can view:

- List of all invited friends
- Their progress (whether they completed the setup or not)
- Whether your reward is unlocked

This helps you keep track of who has completed the required steps to earn the reward.

If no one has joined yet, you will see:

**“No referrals yet. Start inviting friends!”**

## 4. Best Practices for Higher Rewards\*\*

- Share your link with classmates, colleagues, or friends starting their job-ready journey.
- Encourage them to complete their profile so both of you unlock Pro features.
- Post your link on your social accounts for maximum reach.

## **FAQs**

<Expandable title="What counts as a successful referral?">
  A referral is successful when your friend:

  - Signs up using your referral link
  - Completes their full profile setup
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="When will I receive my free month of Pro?">
  Your reward is added automatically as soon as the invited user completes their setup.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I invite multiple friends?">
  Yes! You can invite as many friends as you like and earn multiple rewards.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="My friend forgot to use the link. Can I still get the reward?">
  No. They must sign up **through your invite link** for it to count.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Where can I see my earned credits?">
  Go to: **Profile Icon → Refer Friends → Rewards Tab**
</Expandable>
