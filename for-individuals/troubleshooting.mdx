## **1. Profile Not Updating / Data Not Saving**

**Possible Reasons:**

- Slow or unstable internet
- Session expired
- Required fields not filled
- File format not supported

**How to Fix:**

- Refresh the page and try again
- Re-login to your account
- Make sure all required fields have valid inputs
- For file uploads: use **PDF, PNG, JPG** and keep size under the limit
- Use desktop for best experience

## **2. Skill Badges Not Showing in Skill Repository**

**Why it happens:**

- Assessment not submitted
- Endorsement pending
- YouTube playlist not completed
- Badge rules not satisfied

**Fix:**

- Complete the assessment fully
- Ensure the collaborator or supervisor endorses your skills
- For YouTube: finish 100% of the playlist
- Wait 2–5 minutes for the system to sync

## **3. AI Interview Issues**

### **Interview stuck, paused, or frozen**

**Fix:**

- Check internet speed (AI interviews require a stable connection)
- Do **NOT refresh** the page — refreshing restarts the interview from Question 1
- Close background apps using your camera or microphone
- Use a laptop **(Mobile AI interviews are not supported)**
- Keep good lighting and clear audio

### **Camera/Mic Not Working**

**Fix:**

- Allow browser permissions for camera + microphone
- Restart browser
- Change the camera source in system settings

## **4. Resume Not Downloading / Template Broken**

**Fix:**

- Update your browser to the latest version
- Ensure your profile sections are filled: Education, Work, Projects, Certifications
- Select at least one skill before generating
- Switch between **ATS** and **Visual Template**
- Clear cache if formatting looks incorrect

## **5. Job Tab Locked / Cannot Apply for Jobs**

**Why it happens:**

Job tab unlocks only when your profile meets minimum requirements.

**Required to unlock Jobs:**

- Education
- Work Experience
- Certificates
- Projects
- YouTube Playlist
- 12 Skill Badges
- AI Interview completed

**Fix:**

- Complete all profile checklist items
- Earn at least **2 matching skills** for each job to apply

## **YouTube Playlist Not Converting to Course**

**Fix:**

- Ensure video visibility is not set to private
- Playlist must have at least 1 valid video
- Try reconnecting YouTube integration
- Refresh the Learnings page

## **7. Slow Loading Pages**

**Fix:**

- Check your network
- Use updated Browser
- Close heavy tabs
- Clear cache
