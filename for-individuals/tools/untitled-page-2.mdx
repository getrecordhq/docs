---
title: "FAQs"
---

<Expandable title="What tools are available on Record?">
  Record offers various tools to enhance your profile and help you land the right job. The key tools include:

  - **YouTube to Course**: Convert YouTube content into courses.
  - **One Click Resume**: Generate personalized resumes based on your profile and selected skills.
  - **AI Interview**: Take AI-powered mock interviews and receive feedback on your performance.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How can I create and update my profile on Record?">
  To create a profile, simply fill in your basic details (name, contact, etc.), and add sections like education, work experience, certifications, and skills. You can edit your profile anytime from the **Profile** section to make updates as you grow in your career.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What should I do if I face issues while using any tool?">
  If you face issues with any tool, first ensure your internet connection is stable. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page or restarting the tool. For technical issues like video/audio not working during an interview, ensure your microphone and camera are set up correctly and permissions are granted.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I pause or skip the AI Interview?">
  No, the AI Interview cannot be paused or skipped. If interrupted, refresh the page to start the interview from the beginning. Ensure you are in a quiet environment with a stable internet connection for uninterrupted use.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How does the One Click Resume tool work?">
  Select the relevant skills for the job you are applying for, and the One Click Resume tool will generate a tailored resume automatically. You can choose between an ATS-friendly format or a visual resume based on the job role.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What happens if I fail the AI Interview?">
  If you fail the interview, you will have to wait for a cooldown period (24–48 hours) before you can retake the interview. Use this time to review feedback and improve before retrying.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I add skills to my profile or resume?">
  You can add skills to your profile under the **Skills** section. If you want a skill to be added to your resume, simply select it from the options available in the One Click Resume tool.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I earn a certificate after completing a course?">
  After completing a course (such as from YouTube content), take the assessment linked with the course. Upon scoring 80% or above, you will be awarded a certificate and a skill badge.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I share my profile or resume with others?">
  Yes, you can share your profile or resume by using the sharing options provided within the **Profile** and **One Click Resume** sections. You can also share your interview results with recruiters directly from your profile.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How long do I need to wait to retake the AI Interview if I fail?">
  After failing an AI interview, you will have to wait for a cooldown period of 24–48 hours before retaking the interview. This allows you time to reflect on your performance and improve.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I upload external certificates or courses?">
  Yes, you can add certificates and licenses to your profile by entering the details, such as certificate name, issuing organization, and credential URL, in the **License & Certificates** section.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What happens if I don’t have access to a webcam for the AI Interview?">
  A webcam is required for the AI Interview. If you do not have access to a webcam, you will not be able to take the interview. Make sure your camera is working and properly set up before starting.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Will I be able to download my generated resume?">
  Yes, once your resume is generated using the **One Click Resume** tool, you can download it in PDF format. Choose from the available templates and download it directly to your computer.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I get a certificate after completing the YouTube Course?">
  After completing the YouTube Course and passing the associated assessment with 80% or above, you will receive a certificate of completion along with a skill badge.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I connect my profile with GitHub, Dribbble, or LeetCode?">
  You can connect your GitHub, Dribbble, or LeetCode profiles by entering your username or profile URL in the **Integrations** section under your profile settings. This allows you to showcase your repositories, designs, and coding skills.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Is mobile access available for all tools?">
  Currently, the **AI Interview** tool is not optimized for mobile devices. For best performance, use a desktop or laptop with a stable internet connection and webcam. Other tools like **YouTube to Course** and **One Click Resume** can be accessed via mobile.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I customize the resume template?">
  The **One Click Resume** tool provides pre-designed templates for ATS-friendly and visual resumes. While you cannot fully customize the templates, you can update the content and select which skills, experiences, and education sections to include.
</Expandable>
