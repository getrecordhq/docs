### **Sign up for Record**

You can [sign up for Record app](https://app.getrecord.in/singup) with your Google account ([**Google SSO**](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/12849458)) or Email+Password

**Verify Email:** You will receive an one-time password to verify your email if you sign up with email+password.

**About Section**

The **About** section helps you introduce yourself to recruiters and showcase your professional identity.

<Info>
  This section builds the foundation of your profile and improves how well opportunities match your background.
</Info>

## **Profile Summary**

A brief introduction about who you are, what you’re studying or working on, and the kind of roles you’re targeting.

### Why it matters

- It’s the **first thing** recruiters read.
- Helps recruiters understand your direction and interests.
- Sets context for your skills, projects, and experience.

### What to include

Share a introduction that covers your Educational background, key skills or interests, Work and career details, and any notable achievements.

## **Email Address**

Your primary contact email, used for notifications, updates, and verification.

<Info>
  Used for account verification, job-related updates, and recruiter communication (if visibility is enabled). Use an email you check daily.
</Info>

## **Phone Number**

A direct way for recruiters to reach you if they are interested in your profile.

<Info>
  Adds trust to your profile, supports interview communication, and helps with account recovery.
</Info>

## **Date of Birth**

Used to understand your age group and eligibility for specific programs or roles.

<Info>
  Helps with eligibility for certain fresher or internship roles, personalizes your job recommendations, and keeps your profile records accurate.
</Info>

## **Gender**

Your gender identity.

### Why we collect this

- Some companies run diversity-driven hiring programs
- Improves the accuracy of job matching
- Helps recruiters looking for diversity-focused candidates

## **Marital Status**

Your current marital status.

<Info>
  Sometimes required during pre-onboarding and for relocation/benefit eligibility, but it does not affect your job recommendations.
</Info>

## **Address / Location**

Your current city and state.

<Info>
  Helps recruiters filter by location, match you to city-specific roles, and improve the accuracy of job recommendations use your current active location.
</Info>

## **Languages**

Languages you can speak, read, or write.

<Info>
  Helps recruiters identify you for customer-facing or regional roles, understand your communication strengths, and match you to multilingual opportunities.
</Info>

## **Social Profiles**

External links where your work or online presence can be viewed.

### Common platforms

- LinkedIn
- GitHub
- Behance
- Portfolio Website
- Instagram
- Dribbble

<Info>
  Builds credibility, showcases your real work, and helps recruiters understand your skills beyond what's listed on your profile
</Info>

## **Recruiter Visibility**

A toggle that decides whether recruiters can discover your profile.

### Why enable it

Increases your visibility, makes your profile searchable, and lets recruiters reach out directly turn it off if you prefer a private profile.

## **Purpose of the About Section**

This section helps:

- Build trust through verified details
- Create a complete professional identity
- Improve job discovery
- Personalize recommendations
- Strengthen your recruiter-facing profile

A detailed About section increases your chances of getting noticed.
