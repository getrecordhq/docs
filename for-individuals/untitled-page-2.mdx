title: "General FAQ"
---

<Expandable title="What is Record and how does it help me?">
  Record helps you build a verified skill identity by collecting your education, work experience, projects, certifications, and learning progress. You earn skill badges that prove your abilities and unlock job opportunities.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I unlock the Jobs tab?">
  You need to complete your profile setup:

  - Add Education
  - Add Work Experience
  - Upload Certifications
  - Add Projects
  - Complete a YouTube Playlist
  - Earn 12 Skill Badges
  - Finish the AI Interview

  Once these are done, the Jobs tab unlocks automatically.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Why are skill badges required for job applications?">
  Badges are verified proof of your skills. Recruiters filter candidates based on badges instead of text-based claims. You need **at least 2 matching badges** to apply for any job.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I earn skills?">
  You can earn skills through:

  - AI skill assessments
  - Endorsements
  - Completing YouTube learning
  - Completing courses or certifications
  - Finishing projects and work experiences with mapped skills
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Why can’t I modify the resume template?">
  Resume templates cannot be edited manually.

  All resume details come **directly from your profile**, ensuring accuracy and standardization.

  If you want to change something, update your profile sections.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="My AI interview is not working, what should I do?">
  The AI interview requires:

  - Stable internet connection
  - Laptop device (mobile interviews are not supported)
  - Browser camera and mic permissions

  If the interview freezes:

  - **Do NOT refresh** (it will restart the interview from question 1)
  - Check your network and close background tabs
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Why am I not eligible for some jobs?">
  Each job requires a minimum number of **verified skill badges**.

  If you do not have the required matching skills, Smart Apply will prevent you from applying until you upgrade your skill profile.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Why is my YouTube playlist not converting to a course?">
  Check the following:

  - Playlist is public or unlisted
  - Videos are accessible
  - YouTube integration is active
  - Try reconnecting YouTube if syncing fails
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I change my email or password?">
  Go to **Profile Icon → Settings → Account**

  You can update your email, password, Google login, and other account details there.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What happens when I delete my account?">
  Deleting your account is **permanent**.

  All your data, badges, resume, and interview history will be removed and cannot be recovered.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Is my data secure?">
  Yes. Record uses encrypted systems and secure cloud infrastructure to protect your profile, documents, and interview recordings.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I contact support?">
  Click **Support** in the sidebar or use the help widget.

  Our team will respond as soon as possible.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Why is my profile score low even after filling sections?">
  Profile score improves when you:

  - Add skill-relevant items
  - Earn badges
  - Complete playlists
  - Take the AI interview
  - Add proof-based activities

  The stronger your proof of skill, the higher your score.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What browser should I use for Record?">
  For the best experience, use:

  - **Google Chrome (Recommended)**
  - Microsoft Edge and much more.
</Expandable>
