<Update label="21/11/2025">
  ### 1. New Candidate Profile Experience

  We’ve launched a redesigned candidate profile to help users better showcase their information.\
  The new profile includes:

  - A cleaner and more structured layout
  - Additional required fields for better completeness
  - Improved profile building experience

  This ensures profiles are more useful for recruiters and easier for candidates to manage.

  ### 2. Billing Credits for Upskilling Companies

  Upskilling companies can now use a credit-based system instead of making payments each time they issue skills.

  - Purchase and store credits in advance
  - Use credits anytime when issuing skills
  - Reduces friction during issuance

  This makes the process faster and more scalable.

  ### 3. Referral Program for Candidates

  Candidates can now invite friends and unlock Pro features.

  - Share invite links بسهولة
  - Earn rewards when friends join and complete setup
  - Track referral status and rewards

  This helps grow the community while rewarding users.

  ### 4. Dashboard Notifications for Skill Issuance

  Candidates will now receive notifications when skills are issued to them.

  - Instant alerts on the dashboard
  - Ensures no missed claims
  - Improves visibility of new skills

  ### 5. Updated Candidate Dashboard UI

  We’ve refreshed the candidate dashboard with improved visuals.

  - More engaging and modern design
  - Better layout and readability
  - Enhanced overall experience

  ### 6. Easy Feedback Submission

  Users can now quickly share feedback directly from the platform.

  - Accessible from the bottom-left menu
  - Instant notification sent to the team
  - Faster response and issue resolution

  ### 7. Redesigned Certificates with Public Sharing

  Certificates from learning (including YouTube-based courses) have been redesigned.

  - New, improved certificate design
  - Public verification link for each certificate
  - Easy sharing with recruiters and others
</Update>

<Update label="26/07/2025">
  ### YouTube to course converter, one-click resume, AI interview, and more…

  ### **YouTube to Course Converter is LIVE (YEAH!)**

  After weeks of wrangling APIs, transcript parsing, and assessment logic, we shipped YEAH -YouTube Educational Assessment Hub.

  You can now turn any educational YouTube video or playlist into a distraction-free, structured course.

  Watch → Take notes → Hit 80% → Unlock assessment → Get certified → Earn skill badges.

  Because your self-learning deserves tangible outcomes.

  ### **One-Click Resume**

  Your profile → Your skills → Instant resume. We now generate:

  Pure ATS-friendly resumes tailored to your skills

  Visually branded Record-style resumes too

  Both are downloadable in one click, no formatting stress required.
